Inspiration

This site was made as a fanpage for AP Computer Science A (I). The site's layout is based on Mojang's official Minecraft website.

About Minecraft

Minecraft was started by Markus "Notch" Persson as a small indie game title. Minecraft's popularity grew and attracted major attention from game awards, magazines, websites, etc. However, most of Minecraft's publicity comes from word of mouth. Mojang has never publicly advertised Minecraft and yet over 8,000,000 players have bought the game. Mojang plans on developing Minecraft until interest lowers and then releasing it as open-source, but it doesn't look like that will happen any time soon. Mojang regularly sells over 10,000 copies of the game every day.
